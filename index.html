Other prominent gods in Norse mythology

Frigg is Odin's wife and the queen of the Aesir. She is associated with motherhood, marriage, and domesticity.

Thor is Odin's son and the god of thunder. He is known for his great strength and bravery, often wielding his mighty hammer, Mjolnir.

Baldur is another son of Odin and is considered the god of light, purity, and beauty. His death and eventual resurrection are significant events in Norse mythology.
